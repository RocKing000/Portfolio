# The Hidden Chronicles: Majuli

Original screenplay series grounded in independent research into Assamese tribal culture, Majuli's geography, and Vaishnavite history.

---

## Research Base

The series draws on primary research across three communities — Mising, Deori, and Sonowal Kachari — and covers Majuli's role as a centre of Vaishnavite monastic tradition (the Satras), its geography as a river island, and the cultural dynamics of communities living alongside the Brahmaputra.
