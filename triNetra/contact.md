## What to Include in Your First Message

Describe the system you are working within, not the solution you are looking for. The structural nature of the problem is more useful than a framed solution request: what the system is doing that it should not be, or what it is not doing that it should.

For Eagle Insight Platform (EIP) inquiries, state the domain, the deployment context, and whether you are assessing a single system (EIP) or evaluating against institutional criteria (Eagle Strategic Suite (ESS) or Eagle Portfolio Suite (EPS)).
