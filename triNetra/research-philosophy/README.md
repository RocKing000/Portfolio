# Research Philosophy

This section documents the philosophical foundations of triNetra's research approach — why structured reasoning matters, what distinguishes research from assessment, what separates an Insight Platform from a consultancy, and the first principles from which the methodology is derived.

These are not positioning statements. They are the commitments that govern what triNetra will and will not produce, and why.
