# triNetra

> Research begins where understanding ends.

triNetra is a research ecosystem developing domain-specific Insight Platforms. Through independent research and structured methodologies, its platforms enable structured reasoning across complex systems, transforming structured evidence into decision-grade insights.

---

## Platforms

triNetra develops one Insight Platform at a time, grounded in domain research before launch. Eagle Insight Platform is the first.

```
triNetra Research Ecosystem
│
├── Eagle Insight Platform          ← Active
│   ├── Organisation Intelligence
│   ├── Alignment Intelligence
│   └── Portfolio Intelligence
│
├── Energy Insight Platform         ← Research phase
├── Manufacturing Insight Platform  ← Research phase
├── Infrastructure Insight Platform ← Research phase
└── Defence Insight Platform        ← Research phase
```

See [ecosystem/](ecosystem/) for the full platform map and research directions.  
See [platforms/](platforms/) for full documentation of Eagle Insight Platform.
