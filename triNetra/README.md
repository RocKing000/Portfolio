# triNetra

> Research begins where understanding ends.

triNetra is a research ecosystem developing domain-specific Insight Platforms. Through independent research and structured methodologies, its platforms enable structured reasoning across complex systems, transforming structured evidence into decision-grade insights.

**Eagle Insight Platform** is triNetra's platform for AI and Organisational Intelligence.

---

## What Eagle Insight Platform Delivers

Eagle Insight Platform operates across three intelligence domains. Each domain is a distinct set of deliverables. Navigate directly to what is relevant to you.

| Domain | What It Answers | Available In |
|---|---|---|
| [Organisation Intelligence](organisation-intelligence/) | What exists. What works. What breaks under pressure. | EIP · ESS · EPS |
| [Alignment Intelligence](alignment-intelligence/) | Are we aligned. Where are the gaps. What diverges. | ESS · EPS |
| [Portfolio Intelligence](portfolio-intelligence/) | How the portfolio performs as a system. Correlated risks. | EPS |
