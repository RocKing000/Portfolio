# Ecosystem

triNetra is a research ecosystem. The ecosystem produces domain-specific Insight Platforms. Each platform applies the shared Patterns as a Framework (PaaF) methodology and Eagle Framework to a distinct domain of high-consequence decision-making.

Eagle Insight Platform (EIP) is the first. The platforms below are in the research phase. Domain research is active, the Eagle Framework is being adapted to each domain's structural requirements, and founding partner engagement is available before each launch.

## Why an Ecosystem and Not a Single Platform

A single platform optimised for one domain produces a commercial product. A research ecosystem produces an architecture. That architecture can extend into new domains as research matures, without requiring a new methodology or a new scoring system for each extension.

The shared foundation (Eagle Framework, PaaF, TPRS) means that every new platform benefits from what every prior platform learned. A finding in the energy domain about epistemic uncertainty under grid-scale partial observability enriches the Eagle Framework's understanding of partial observability as a structural condition. This benefits future assessments in every domain.

This is the compound research advantage of an ecosystem model over a product model.
