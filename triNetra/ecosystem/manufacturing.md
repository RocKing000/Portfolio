# Manufacturing Insight Platform

**Status:** Research phase  
**Domain:** Manufacturing systems, production intelligence

---

> Structural assessment for manufacturing systems and production intelligence.

---

## Purpose

Manufacturing Insight Platform will apply the Eagle Framework to AI and decision systems operating in manufacturing contexts — process management, quality assurance, supply chain decision-making, and production optimisation.

Manufacturing systems are structurally distinct from information systems: physical consequences of AI decision failures are immediate, irreversible, and often invisible until they propagate through the production chain. A quality system that misclassifies defects does not produce an incorrect data point. It produces defective product at scale, liability exposure, and supply chain disruption. The structural conditions that allow this to happen are assessable before the failure occurs.

The Manufacturing Insight Platform is being built to make those conditions visible and measurable.

---

## Target Organisations

- Manufacturers deploying AI in quality assurance, process control, and production optimisation
- Supply chain managers using AI in procurement and supplier decision systems
- Industrial organisations seeking governance-grade auditability for AI systems operating under ISO and regulatory requirements
- Institutional investors assessing AI-intensive manufacturing operations

---

## Research Direction

The platform's research is focusing on:

- Process integrity under partial observability in automated production systems
- Quality system architecture and the structural conditions for defect classification failure
- Supply chain decision systems under conflicting signals and adversarial input
- Operational resilience of AI systems under production constraint and material uncertainty
