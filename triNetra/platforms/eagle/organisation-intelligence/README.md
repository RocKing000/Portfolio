# Organisation Intelligence

> What exists. What works. What breaks under pressure. Where does improvement matter most.

Organisation Intelligence is the foundational domain of the Eagle Insight Platform (EIP). It evaluates a deployed system across seven dimensions of the Eagle Framework and produces a complete Assessment Report with an Eagle Score, maturity level classification, and a structured remediation roadmap.

Available in the EIP, Eagle Strategic Suite (ESS), and Eagle Portfolio Suite (EPS) bundles.

---

## What It Answers

- What is the current structural state of this system?
- Where does it work, and where does it fail under pressure?
- What are the highest-priority structural gaps?
- How mature is this system against the Eagle auditability standard?

## The Assessed System

Organisation Intelligence applies to any deployed system operating in a high-consequence domain where the quality of decision-making has structural consequences. The Eagle Framework covers 30 canonical high-risk domains across six super-domains: Infrastructure, Economic, Human Safety, Governance, Intelligence, and Physical World.

See [domain-manifest-library.md](../eagle-framework/domain-manifest-library.md) for the full domain catalogue.
