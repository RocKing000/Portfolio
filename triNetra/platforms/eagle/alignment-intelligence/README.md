# Alignment Intelligence

> Are we aligned. Where are the gaps. What diverges. What needs to evolve.

Alignment Intelligence enables institutional clients to define their investment criteria and structural expectations once — then evaluate portfolio companies against that standard consistently across every assessment cycle.

Available in the ESS and EPS bundles.

---

## What It Answers

- Does this portfolio company meet our structural criteria?
- Where are the specific gaps between what we require and what exists?
- What structural divergences are present across the portfolio?
- How has alignment changed since the last cycle?

## Who It Is For

Institutional investors, venture capital firms, and asset managers who deploy capital into organisations operating AI systems in high-consequence environments and require a consistent, evidence-grounded standard for evaluating alignment across their portfolio.
