# Licensing: Eagle Strategic Suite (ESS)

The Eagle Strategic Suite (ESS) bundle provides access to Organisation Intelligence and Alignment Intelligence. It is designed for institutional clients assessing portfolio companies against a defined structural standard.

---

## What Is Included

- Organisation Intelligence for each assessed portfolio company (Eagle Score, maturity level, Assessment Report)
- Reference Manifest definition and management
- Alignment Intelligence per portfolio company (Alignment Score, Gap Analysis, Structural Divergence Analysis)
- Reference Manifest versioning across cycles
