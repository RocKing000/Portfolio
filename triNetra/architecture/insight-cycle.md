# Insight Cycle

The Insight Cycle is the operational unit of Eagle Insight Platform (EIP) engagement. Each cycle covers a defined assessment period and produces a complete set of deliverables for the intelligence domains included in the client's bundle.

---

## What an Insight Cycle Is

An Insight Cycle begins when the assessed entity submits an Input Manifest. It closes when the Assessment Report and all comparative deliverables for that cycle are delivered.

The deliverables produced within a single Insight Cycle depend on the client's bundle:

| Bundle | Inputs | Deliverables |
|---|---|---|
| Organisation Intelligence (EIP) | One Input Manifest submission | Assessment Report, Eagle Score, maturity level classification |
| Alignment Intelligence (Eagle Strategic Suite (ESS)) | Input Manifests for each assessed portfolio company | Assessment Report per company, Alignment Score, Gap Analysis, Structural Divergence Analysis relative to the Reference Manifest |
| Portfolio Intelligence (Eagle Portfolio Suite (EPS)) | All ESS inputs | All ESS deliverables plus cross-portfolio structural pattern analysis, correlated risk map, and portfolio structural evolution tracking |

---

## Cadence

EIP clients bill quarterly. Each quarter is one Insight Cycle. An EIP client may run one assessment per cycle or more, depending on engagement scope.

ESS and EPS clients bill annually. The annual engagement structures assessment cycles around the institutional client's portfolio review calendar.

---

## Cycle-to-Cycle Continuity

The Insight Cycle model compounds in value across cycles. The Reference Manifest (ESS and EPS) carries forward and can be versioned as the investment thesis evolves. Historical Eagle Scores and maturity levels from prior cycles form the basis for Evolution Analysis. Evolution Analysis tracks whether the system is improving, stable, or deteriorating structurally over time.

A client who runs three consecutive Insight Cycles gains a structural history. That history answers what the system was, what changed, what improved, and what structural conditions persist despite intervention. This history is not available to clients of point-in-time assessment tools.

---

## Research Artifacts Within a Cycle

The following optional deliverables are available within an active Insight Cycle:

| Artifact | What It Produces | Price |
|---|---|---|
| Change Analysis | Structural comparison between two Input Manifest versions with projected score change | USD 99 per submission |
| Evolution Analysis | Structural progression tracking across a calendar day | USD 249 per day |
| Executive Intelligence Dossier | Executive-level structured intelligence across a calendar week | USD 999 per week |

Research Artifacts are optional and do not affect assessment execution. They are ordered independently during an active cycle.
