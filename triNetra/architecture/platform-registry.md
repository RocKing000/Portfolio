# Platform Registry

The triNetra platform registry is the authoritative record of all Insight Platforms — active, research-phase, and planned. It defines what each platform is, what domain it addresses, what its current status is, and what research is required before it can launch.

---

## What a Platform Is

In the triNetra architecture, a platform is a complete, independently operable research instrument for a specific domain of high-consequence decision-making. A platform is considered complete when it has:

1. A domain-specific manifest library — structured input templates covering the full taxonomy of decision types, evidence types, and threat domains within the target domain
2. A calibrated scoring architecture — dimension weightings and epistemic priors appropriate to the domain's uncertainty characteristics
3. A regulatory alignment mapping — correspondence between Eagle Framework dimensions and the regulatory frameworks that govern the domain
4. A defined intelligence domain structure — the specific Organisation / Alignment / Portfolio configuration that applies to the domain's institutional buyers
5. An active research programme — ongoing research that evolves the platform as the domain evolves

A configuration of a general-purpose tool is not a platform. A platform is derived from domain-specific research.

---

## Platform Readiness Criteria

A research direction enters the registry when there is sufficient evidence that:

- The domain has structural assessment requirements that the Eagle Framework can address
- The domain has institutional buyers who need independent, evidence-grounded assessment
- The regulatory or governance environment creates demand for structured assessment

A registered platform moves from research phase to active when:

- The domain manifest library is complete for at least ten canonical domain configurations
- The scoring architecture is calibrated using domain-specific test cases
- At least one founding partner engagement has been completed

## Founding Partner Engagement for Research-Phase Platforms

Founding partner engagement is available for research-phase platforms before launch. A founding partner engagement during the research phase contributes domain-specific evidence to the platform's manifest library and calibration. Founding partners receive access to the platform at founding rates when it launches.

Contact research@trinetra.life with the subject line specifying the platform of interest.
