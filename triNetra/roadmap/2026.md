# 2026: Founding Period

## Eagle Insight Platform: Current Access

**Access:** Eagle Insight Platform is currently available through the Founding Validation Programme (invitation-only, until 01 January 2027).

| Item | Price | Notes |
|---|---|---|
| Platform Access | USD 3,999 | One-time. Covers onboarding, Input Manifest template, and access to the triNetra Research Studio. |
| Insight Cycle | USD 999 | Per engagement. Each Insight Cycle produces a full Assessment Report across all seven Eagle Framework dimensions. |
| Founding Partner Reservation | Optional | Locks founding rates for post-2027 enterprise licensing. |

Enterprise bundles (EIP, ESS, EPS) are available from 01 January 2027. See [2027.md](2027.md) for standard and founding rates.
