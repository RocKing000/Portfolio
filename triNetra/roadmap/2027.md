## Research Direction

The triNetra research programme in 2027 will be informed by the findings from Eagle Insight Platform's founding period. The specific research directions will depend on what the founding assessments reveal about structural assessment requirements that the Eagle Framework does not yet fully address.

Confirmed research directions:

**Biotech Insight Platform development.** The Biotech Domain study currently in progress is the research foundation for a Biotech Insight Platform. If the study's findings confirm Eagle Framework applicability to the biotech domain, platform development begins in 2027.

**Domain manifest library expansion.** The current 30-domain manifest library will be extended based on assessment demand from founding partners. New domains are added when sufficient evidence exists from real assessments to calibrate the domain-specific parameters.

**Cross-platform methodology refinement.** Findings from Eagle Insight Platform assessments will be used to refine the Eagle Framework dimensions for the research-phase platforms. Epistemic priors, utility bounds, and weighting parameters will be adapted for energy, manufacturing, infrastructure, and defence domain characteristics.

---

## Ecosystem Platforms

The research-phase platforms (Energy, Manufacturing, Infrastructure, Defence) continue domain research in 2027. The first post-Eagle platform launch date will be announced based on research maturity, not a fixed schedule.

The criterion for launch is research readiness: the domain manifest library must be complete for at least ten canonical domain configurations, the scoring architecture must be calibrated using domain-specific test cases, and at least one founding partner engagement must have been completed.
